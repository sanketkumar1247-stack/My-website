Biography

About

I am a PhD Scholar at the Centre for European Studies, School of International Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. My work sits at the intersection of European Union external action, digital regulation, and the global politics of norms.

Profile

Before joining JNU for doctoral work, I completed an M.A. and a B.A. (Hons.) in Political Science at the University of Delhi, and higher secondary studies at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Morena. I hold a UGC Junior Research Fellowship and NET, and have taught Indian Polity for UPSC and MPPSC aspirants remotely.

I also serve as a peer reviewer for the Journal of Applied Social Sciences (SAGE) and completed Oxford’s online course on Freedom of Expression and Safety of Journalism. Languages: Hindi (native), English (professional).
